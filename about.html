About Me




Hello! I'm Subhodeep Baroi, a passionate and dedicated Python developer with a strong background in modern computer applications. With a high school education focused on these applications, I've developed a solid foundation in the technical skills required for software development and cybersecurity.

I'm excited about the opportunities ahead and look forward to collaborating with like-minded professionals to create impactful software.

Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you'd like to discuss potential collaborations or opportunities.
